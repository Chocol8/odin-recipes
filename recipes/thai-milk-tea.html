Thai Milk Tea Recipe

A simple thai iced tea recipe using thai tea mix tea bags from ChaTraMue. This recipe creates 1 serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 thai mix tea bag/li>
  • 100 grams fresh milk
  • 10 grams condesnsed milk
  1. Brew tea bag in 100 grams hot water for 10 minutes
  2. Remove tea bag and add condensed milk. Stir until condensed milk is incorporated in drink./li>
  3. Add 100 grams cold water and 100 grams milk.
  4. Serve with ice

Iced Tea Recipe

A simple iced tea recipe using black tea. This recipe created 2 liters of iced tea.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups water
  • 8 black tea bags
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup lemon juice
  1. Heat 8 cups of water until boiling
  2. Add 8 tea bags and allow to brew in low heat for 8 minutes
  3. Remove tea bags and add sugar and lemon juice. Stir until sugar is dissolved, and wait for iced tea to cool
  4. Add into container and refrigerate to cool
  5. Optional: add lemon slices when serving
